NI Game Dev x Base Arcade

Hi game devs!

TLDR: We're running an arcade event in collaboration with NI Science Festival at Banana Block next month and we think it'd be really cool if you could be part of it.

Over Valentines weekend (14-16 Feb) we think it'd be really cool to bring a local flavour our regular arcade event by showcasing what's happening in game dev right on our doorstep.

Our thought was: alongside the regular arcade event that it'd be cool to have a casual "play my demo" style section, where indie devs and studios could showcase their games to people attending the arcade as part of NI Science fest.

Our audience ranges from families with young kids during the day, to couples and groups of friends on a night out — so might be a bit different to the people you normally get to show your games to. We usually bring in around 500-550 people over the weekend across 5 different sessions in the 3 days.

We think this could be a fun way for you to get feedback from a more casual gaming audience and for the arcade goers to enjoy something a bit different to our normal event set up.

It’s worth pointing out that we don’t expect people to only bring polished or complete games — the whole point of this is to connect people with the local scene, to showcase the interesting things happening locally and primarily for you to get some utility from the event.

We’ll be honest, we haven’t figured out anything like floor plans or exact set up logistics yet — that will likely depend on numbers. But the goal is to keep this casual and low effort for everyone.

The number of people interested in showcasing will likely dictate when and how we go about it. For example, if only a handful are interested in taking part, we can decide to do this in a single 4 hour session.

If the interest is greater, we can maybe spread this out over a few different sessions or days.

Firstly, we’d like to gauge if there’s any interest in coming along to showcase in the first place. So, if you’re interested, stick your name down on this Google form and we’ll take it from there.
